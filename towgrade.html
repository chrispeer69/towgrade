Key findings & recommended actions
F-01
Payment infrastructure failing at scale
Average payment windows have stretched from 28 to 38 days over 12 months. Three of the five largest providers now routinely exceed 45 days, creating cash flow crises for small fleet operators. Recommended: mandate 21-day payment SLAs with financial penalties in all provider contracts.
F-02
Digital platforms significantly underperforming motor clubs
App-based platforms score 1.9 points lower than motor clubs on average across all categories. Technology investment is not keeping pace with dispatch volume. Recommended: require quarterly reliability reporting and minimum uptime SLAs from digital platform providers.
F-03
Southeast region showing systemic Agero underperformance
Agero scores 1.2 points lower in the Southeast than its national average. Recommended: request regional breakdown in Agero's next contract review and consider region-specific SLAs.
F-04
NSD and AAA demonstrate the viable best-practice model
Both providers consistently score above 7.0 across all categories. Shared characteristics: dedicated account managers, sub-25-day payment windows, and transparent damage claim processes. Recommended: use as the RFP benchmark standard for all future procurement cycles.
